## Executive Summary

**TinySteps** is a comprehensive mobile application designed specifically for fathers whose babies are in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). The app provides emotional support, practical tracking tools, and comprehensive UK-specific resources to help dads navigate one of life's most challenging experiences with confidence and support.

---

## App Overview

### Mission Statement
To provide emotional support, practical assistance, and comprehensive resources for fathers during their baby's NICU stay, helping them feel supported, informed, and empowered throughout their journey.

### Target Audience
- **Primary:** Fathers with babies in the NICU
- **Secondary:** NICU families, partners, and healthcare providers
- **Geographic Focus:** United Kingdom (with comprehensive UK support integration)

---

## Key Features

### 1. Home Dashboard
- Daily encouragement messages tailored for NICU dads
- Quick stats overview (today's feeding, nappy changes, sleep)
- Today's Focus action cards:
  - **Skin-to-Skin Timer:** Full-featured timer with benefits information
  - **Talk to Baby:** Tips and guidance for bonding
  - **Ask Questions:** Question manager that saves to journal
  - **Read Stories:** Story suggestions and reading tips
  - **Daily Progress:** Quick navigation to progress tracking
- UK Support Resources with one-tap calling functionality
- Quick contact cards (NICU Nurse, Doctor, Social Worker) - editable
- Quick journal entry access

### 2. Progress Tracking
- **Vital Metrics:** Weight, height, head circumference tracking
- **Medical Data:** Heart rate (bpm), breathing, temperature, feeding status
- **Visual Charts:** Growth charts and progress visualizations
- **Milestone Tracking:** Physical, Cognitive, Social, and Language milestones
- **Progress History:** Timeline view with edit/delete functionality
- **Smart Date Display:** Shows "Today," "Yesterday," day names, or full dates
- **Real-time Updates:** Dynamic display from saved data

### 3. Daily Journal
- **Private Journaling:** Secure space for thoughts and feelings
- **Photo Attachments:** Capture and attach precious moments
- **Mood Tracking:** Quick mood selection buttons
- **Journal Prompts:** Suggestions to help guide entries
- **Memory Cards:** Special moments and achievements
- **Selection Mode:** Multi-select for bulk deletion
- **Tap to Edit:** Easy editing of entries and memories
- **Quick Journal Bar:** Fast entry creation
- **Mental Health Support:** UK resources integrated throughout

### 4. NICU Information Hub
- **Medical Glossary:** Comprehensive NICU terminology
- **Categories:**
  - Equipment (monitors, ventilators, incubators)
  - Procedures (tests, treatments, surgeries)
  - Conditions (medical conditions and diagnoses)
  - Support (resources and organizations)
- **Dad-Friendly Explanations:** Medical terms explained in accessible language
- **UK Support Integration:** Local resources and contact information

## Data Tracking & Management

### Core Data Models
1. **Baby Profile:** Name, birth date, weight, height, due date, gender, photo
2. **Feeding Records:** Types (Breast, Bottle, Mixed), amount, duration, side tracking
3. **Nappy Records:** Types (Wet, Dirty, Both)
4. **Sleep Records:** Duration, location, quality tracking
5. **Progress Entries:** Weight, height, head circumference, heart rate, breathing, temperature
6. **Milestones:** Physical, Cognitive, Social, Language achievements
7. **Achievements (DadAchievement):** Firsts, Bonding, Care, Support, Learning categories

9. **Health Visitor Visits:** Visit tracking with measurements
