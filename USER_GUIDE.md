## Getting Started

### First Launch
When you first open TinySteps, you'll be greeted with a beautiful onboarding experience that will guide you through setting up your baby's profile.

### Creating Your Baby's Profile
1. **Tap "Get Started"** on the welcome screen
2. **Enter Baby's Name** - This will be displayed throughout the app
3. **Select Birth Date** - Use the date picker to select your baby's birth date
4. **Enter Weight** - Record your baby's birth weight in kg or lbs
5. **Enter Height** - Record your baby's birth height in cm or inches
6. **Choose Feeding Method** - Select from Breast, Bottle, or Mixed
7. **Tap "Save"** to create the profile

### Setting Up Notifications
1. **Go to Settings** (gear icon in the bottom tab bar)
2. **Tap "Notifications"**
3. **Enable Notifications** if prompted by iOS
4. **Configure Reminder Types**:
   - Feeding reminders
   - Sleep schedule alerts
   - Milestone notifications
   - Growth tracking reminders

1. **Tap "Add Sleep"**
2. **Select Start Time** (or tap "Now")
3. **Choose Location**:

   - Bassinet

   - Car
   - Stroller
   - Other
4. **Rate Sleep Quality**:
   - Excellent
   - Good
   - Fair
   - Poor
5. **Add Notes** (optional)
6. **Tap "Save"**

#### Growth Tracking
1. **Tap "Add Weight"** or **"Add Height"**

5. **Tap "Save"**

#### Nappy Tracking
1. **Tap "Add Nappy"**
2. **Select Type**:

## Data Management

### Exporting Data
1. **Go to Settings**
2. **Tap "Data & Privacy"**
3. **Tap "Export Data"**
4. **Choose format**:
   - JSON (for developers)
   - CSV (for spreadsheets)
   - PDF (for printing)
5. **Tap "Export"**
6. **Share** via email, AirDrop, or save to Files

### Backing Up Data
1. **Go to Settings**
2. **Tap "Data & Privacy"**
3. **Tap "Backup Data"**
4. **Choose backup location**:
   - iCloud
   - Local device
5. **Tap "Create Backup"**

### Restoring Data
1. **Go to Settings**
2. **Tap "Data & Privacy"**
3. **Tap "Restore Data"**
4. **Select backup file**
5. **Tap "Restore"**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Open
1. **Force close** the app and reopen
2. **Restart** your device
3. **Check** for app updates
4. **Reinstall** the app if necessary

#### Data Not Saving
1. **Check** device storage space
2. **Restart** the app
3. **Verify** you're logged in
4. **Check** internet connection (for cloud backup)

#### Notifications Not Working
1. **Check** iOS notification settings
2. **Enable** notifications for TinySteps
3. **Verify** notification types are enabled
4. **Check** Do Not Disturb settings

#### Sync Issues
1. **Check** internet connection
2. **Verify** iCloud is enabled
3. **Sign out** and back in
4. **Contact** support if issues persist
