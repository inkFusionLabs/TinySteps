TinySteps overview

Built around the NICU day

  • Home dashboard keeps you grounded with daily vitals, quick encouragement, and shift handovers at a glance.
  • Today's Focus cards surface bonding moments—skin-to-skin, reading, gentle talk—timed to what baby needs.
  • Quick contacts put the nurse station, neonatologist, and social worker one tap away, even offline.
  • Global support shortcuts connect you to trusted parent and mental-health resources across 20+ countries.

What makes TinySteps different

  • Designed with NICU fathers who needed a dedicated space to process, plan, and stay connected.
  • Privacy-first architecture with encrypted storage and no third-party ads—your family story stays yours.
  • Accessibility-first interface supporting VoiceOver, large type, and calming dark mode for low-light wards.

Core tools you’ll rely on

Progress & milestones

Track weight, feeds, oxygen, and milestones with trend views that spotlight the wins your care team is cheering for.

Journal that adapts

Capture quick notes, add photos, and clip daily tips or questions for rounds—timestamped so nothing gets lost.

Information hub

Dad-friendly medical glossary, NHS pathways, and mental health resources curated with neonatal specialists.
