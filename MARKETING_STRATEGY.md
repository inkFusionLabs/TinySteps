## üìä **Market Reality Check**

### Market Size Analysis
- **UK NICU admissions**: ~8,000 premature babies per year
- **Average NICU stay**: 2-4 weeks
- **NICU dads**: ~8,000-10,000 per year
- **Smartphone users**: 95% of UK adults
- **App download rate**: Conservative estimate = 5-10% of NICU dads = 400-1,000 potential users

### Competition Analysis
- **Direct competitors**: Very few NICU-specific apps
- **Indirect competitors**: General baby tracking apps (BabyDaybook, Sprout)
- **Opportunity**: No major competitor specifically for NICU dads
